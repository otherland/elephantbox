About Us

The Story

Elephant Box is the first UK based company to develop a range of stainless steel food containers for the UK & European market. Our company was born from a simple goal. To create products that mean people can stop buying and using plastic.

Back in 2010 an alternative to plastic for storing food was few and far between. Not only did Joy and I know plastic was harmful to both humans and the planet, things made of plastic just didn't last as well. We decided if Joy couldnt find a proper lunchbox for her kids to take to school, then we’d better go ahead and get one manufactured. Elephant Box was born...

The Sustainable Solution

Elephant Box - Big and beautiful, the product that got the whole company started. Its a lunchbox for a proper packed lunch. Elephant Box also a great food storage box and cleverly designed to stack, so it's great for keeping all those kitchen essentials. Use a marker pen to personalise and keep track of what’s inside!

Stainless steel is naturally BPA and phlalate free. Unlike some plastics this means no chemicals can leach into your food or drinks. So it’s safer for you and your family.

Stainless steel is easy to clean and stays that way it's whole life. No staining or odours.

Our products are tough and durable. They will be with you for years. They won’t break, and you won't throw them away.
